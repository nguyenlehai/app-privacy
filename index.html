Privacy Policy

Applies to all applications developed by Hai Nguyen Le

This Privacy Policy applies to all iOS and Android applications developed and published by Hai Nguyen Le on the App Store and Google Play. Apps may use different business models — free, ad-supported, or with in-app purchases / subscriptions — and this policy covers all of those cases.

Last updated: June 06, 2026

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on the specific app, we may collect the following types of data:

a) Anonymous technical data (all apps)

  • Crash reports — technical error information (device type, OS version, stack trace) to help us fix bugs. Does not contain personally identifiable information.
  • Anonymous usage statistics — app opens, features used, session duration. Not linked to personal identity.

b) Notification data (apps with push notifications)

  • Push notification token — a device identifier used solely to deliver in-app notifications. Not shared with third parties for marketing.

c) Advertising data (ad-supported apps only)

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA / Advertising ID) — used by ad networks (Google AdMob) to show relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device Settings.
  • See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how AdMob handles data.

d) Purchase data (apps with in-app purchases or subscriptions)

  • All payment transactions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store credit card or payment information.
  • Purchase history may be stored anonymously to support the restore purchases feature.
We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or precise location unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via a support contact form).

2. How We Use Information

  • Detect and fix technical issues
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send reminders or updates via push notifications (only if you have granted permission)
  • Display relevant advertisements (ad-supported apps only)
  • Process and verify in-app purchases

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. Data is only shared with third parties in these cases:

  • Service providers listed in Section 4 (Firebase, Sentry, AdMob, RevenueCat).
  • When required by law or legal process.

4. Third-Party Services

Depending on the app, we may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • Sentry — crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertising (ad-supported apps only)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (apps with in-app purchases only)

5. Device Permissions

Apps only request permissions when necessary:

  • Notifications — reminders and updates (optional)
  • Calendar — adding events to the system calendar (optional)
  • Camera / Photo Library — some apps use this for content input (optional)
  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — iOS will prompt separately; you may decline

All permissions can be revoked at any time in Settings → [App Name] on your device.

6. Data Storage

User preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. Anonymous technical data is stored on third-party service providers' servers and deleted according to their respective retention policies.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect information. However, no method of Internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children

Unless explicitly stated in an app's store listing, our apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us to have it deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

The app crashes or won't open.

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall from the App Store or Google Play. If it still fails, email us with your device model and OS version.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications → [App Name] on your device and toggle notifications off. You can also adjust this inside the app's Settings screen.

How do I restore a purchase or subscription?

Open the app, go to Settings or the premium screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel a subscription?

iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → select the app → Cancel.
Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → select the app → Cancel.

I have a feature request.

We'd love to hear it! Send an email with the subject "Feature request: [App Name]". We read all feedback and do our best to incorporate popular requests.

How is my data handled?

See our Privacy Policy for full details. In short: we do not collect personal information, and we never sell your data.
